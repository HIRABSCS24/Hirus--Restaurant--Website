About Hirus

More Than a Meal — It's a Memory

Hirus was founded in 2018 with one simple purpose — to bring people together over genuinely delicious food. Nestled in the heart of Karachi, our kitchen serves a curated mix of Pakistani, Italian, Continental and more, made fresh every single day.

Whether you're here for a family dinner, a date night, or a quick lunch with friends — we promise you'll leave happy, full, and already planning your next visit.
